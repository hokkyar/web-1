About Us

We accumulate the best fresh fruit from intensively and wholeheartedly managed fruit plantations in northern Bali. The quality of Bali's fertile soil maintains tree maturity to provide the selected tropical fruit. We are engaged in agribusiness and work with more than 20 selected local gardeners to prepare the most high-grade quality fresh fruit. We proceed to innovate in the marketing process to multiple countries.

Mangosteen

Mangosteen is oftenly called the queen of fruit because it carries a lot of benefits. Mangosteen fruit contains several nutrients with antioxidant capacities, such as vitamin C and folate. In addition, mangosteen fruit can also provide xanthones, a unique type of plant compound known to have powerful antioxidant properties. Mangosteen is grouped into 3, ranging from grade A, grade AA, to grade AAA. Contact us for more details
